# Simplify AI Engineering

**An AI-native learning & interview-prep platform for AI engineering — "Hello Interview, for AI."**

We're building a modern web application that turns the best open AI-engineering
knowledge into an **interactive, AI-native learning and interview-prep platform**:
guided practice, an AI mock interviewer, a grounded AI tutor, a system-design
whiteboard, and personalized study plans — on top of a continuously-updated knowledge
base.

## The thesis (one paragraph)

[aidaddy.tech](https://www.aidaddy.tech) is an excellent but **read-only** reference for
production AI systems and AI-engineering interviews (open-source, MIT-licensed). Classic
prep platforms like [Hello Interview](https://www.hellointerview.com) are **interactive
and AI-native** but barely cover AI-system design. We own the intersection: **interactive,
assessed, AI-native prep for AI-engineering interviews**, on a knowledge base that stays
current. The free reader is the funnel; the AI mock interviewer, grounded tutor, and
AI-specific guided practice are the moat.
